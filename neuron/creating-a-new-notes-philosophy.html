Creating a new notes philosophy
Sridhar Ratnakumar
2020-05-29 14:21:46

Neuron needs a moto. Something like "_Your_ notes, for _life_". The idea is to emphasize two things that distinguishes neuron from other systems:

  1. Notes are, ultimately, stored on _your_ system (be it local computer, or a remote Git repository)
  2. Notes are available for the _entirety of your lifetime_. Even if the author of neuron (me), or the upcoming web app, vanishes into thin air - your notes will remain alive in non-proprietary format (plain-text), and somebody will take over the mainternership of the open source app neuron so you can continue to use it.

I wonder if I should also create a GitHub organization to capture this philosophy (see example). Wonder what a good name for that would be ...
