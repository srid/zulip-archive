I've noticed a lot of projects write their LANGUAGE pragmas:

{-# LANGUAGE Like #-}
{-# LANGUAGE This #-}

instead of

{-# LANGUAGE
    Like
  , This
#-}

Is there a particular reason for doing it the former way, or am I reading too much into it?
